Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Profile
How do I create an account?

Click the "Login/Register" button in the top navigation, then select "Create Account". Fill in your details, choose your favorite genres, and you're ready to join the GameHub community!

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

How do I update my profile information?

After logging in, click on your profile icon in the top right corner, then select "Edit Profile". You can update your username, email, favorite genres, and profile picture from there.

Reviews & Content
How do I write a game review?

Navigate to the Reviews page and scroll to the "Write Your Own Review" section. Fill in the game title, select a platform, give your rating, and write your review. Click "Submit Review" when done.

Can I edit or delete my review?

Yes! Go to your profile page, click on "My Reviews", and you'll see options to edit or delete each review. You can only edit or delete your own reviews.

How are game ratings calculated?

Game ratings are an average of all user ratings, weighted by reviewer credibility. Our expert reviews also influence the overall score. We use a transparent algorithm to ensure fair ratings.

Shopping & Purchases
How do I purchase a game or accessory?

Browse the Shop or Accessories page, select your item, choose platform/store, and click "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase. Digital games are delivered instantly via email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets. Payment processing is secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

What is your refund policy?

Digital games: 14-day refund if not downloaded/activated. Accessories: 30-day return policy for defective items. Physical goods: Return within 30 days in original packaging.

Technical Issues
The website is loading slowly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache, disable browser extensions, or try a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact our support team.

Videos aren't playing. How do I fix this?

Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video. Update your browser to the latest version. Disable any ad blockers or privacy extensions that might be blocking video content.
