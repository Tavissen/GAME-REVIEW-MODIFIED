Community Guidelines

Rules and expectations for our gaming community

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat all members with respect and courtesy
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Respect different opinions and experiences
  • Constructive criticism is welcome, personal attacks are not

2. Quality Discussions

  • Keep discussions game-related and on-topic
  • No spamming or excessive self-promotion
  • Use spoiler tags for major story content
  • Share your experiences and insights

3. Content Rules

  • No NSFW or inappropriate content
  • No piracy, cheating, or hacking discussions
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property
  • No personal information sharing

4. Moderation

  • Moderators have final say in disputes
  • Report violations using the report button
  • Appeals can be made via contact form
  • Rules apply to all members equally

5. Gaming Etiquette

  • No cheating or exploiting glitches in multiplayer
  • Be a good sport, win or lose
  • Help new players learn and improve
  • Share tips and strategies constructively
Important Notice

Violation of these guidelines may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent banning from the GameHub community.
