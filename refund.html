Our Guarantee

At GameHub, we stand behind the quality of our products. We offer a 14-day refund guarantee for all digital game purchases, provided certain conditions are met. Our goal is to ensure you have the best gaming experience possible.

Refund Eligible
  • Game doesn't launch or run
  • Product significantly differs from description
  • Accidental duplicate purchase
  • Technical issues we cannot resolve
Not Eligible
  • Changed your mind after playing
  • Game played for more than 2 hours
  • Purchased more than 14 days ago
  • Violation of terms of service

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to request a refund?

You have 14 days from the date of purchase to request a refund, provided you have played the game for less than 2 hours total.

What information do I need to provide?

Please provide your order number, purchase date, email used for purchase, and a detailed description of the issue you're experiencing.

Will my refund include taxes and fees?

Yes, approved refunds include the full purchase amount including any taxes and fees paid during the original transaction.

Can I refund a game I purchased as a gift?

Gift purchases can be refunded if the gift hasn't been redeemed by the recipient. Once redeemed, the recipient must request the refund.

What if I purchased from a third-party store?

For games purchased through Steam, PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, etc., you must follow their respective refund policies. We can only refund purchases made directly through GameHub.

Special Cases & Exceptions

Important Restrictions
  • Refunds are not available for in-game purchases, DLC, or microtransactions
  • Season passes and subscription services are non-refundable after use
  • Pre-orders can be refunded until the game's release date
  • Bundled games must be refunded as a complete bundle
Pro-Rated Refunds

In some exceptional cases, we may offer partial refunds for games played between 2-4 hours, at our discretion.
