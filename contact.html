Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a game review?
You can submit your review through our review submission form on the Reviews page. Make sure to include the game title, platform, and your detailed thoughts on gameplay, graphics, and story.
Can I upload my game screenshots?
Yes! Visit our Gallery page and use the upload feature to share your game screenshots with the community. Make sure they're high-quality and appropriate.
How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use our live chat feature during business hours.
Do you accept guest reviews?
Yes, we welcome guest reviews from the gaming community. Submit your review through our platform, and if it meets our quality standards, it will be featured on our site.
